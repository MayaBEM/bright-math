How It Works

  • There are 30 multiple-choice questions, each with 4 choices (A, B, C, D).
  • Answer at your own pace — there is no timer.
  • Use Previous and Next to move between questions, or jump using the question map.
  • Challenge Mode: your answers are saved as you go, and you'll see your score after submitting.
  • Practice Mode: check each answer right away and see a short explanation.
  • Press Submit My Answers when you're finished, then explore the full answer review.
  • Missed a few? Use Retry Missed Questions to try just those again.
